This is a known list of all known CVEs relevant for Rack 2.2.17.10 LTS.

- CVE-2022-44571

- CVE-2022-44572
  - ReDos in Rack

- CVE-2023-27530

- CVE-2024-25126
  - ReDoS vulnerability in Rack

- CVE-2024-26141

- CVE-2024-26146
  - ReDoS vulnerability in Rack

- CVE-2025-25184
  - Possible log injection in Rack

- CVE-2025-49007
