This is a known list of all known CVEs relevant for Rack 1.4.7.10 LTS.

  - XSS in Rack

- CVE-2020-8161
  - Directory traversal in Rack

- CVE-2020-8184
  - CSRF in Rack

- CVE-2022-30123
  - Shell escape sequence injection vulnerability in Rack

- CVE-2022-44572
  - ReDos in Rack
